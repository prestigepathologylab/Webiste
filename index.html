Our Services

Service Area

HEMOGLOBIN TEST

A crucial blood test that measures the oxygen-carrying protein in your red blood cells, primarily used to diagnose and monitor anemia.

BLOOD TESTING

The comprehensive analysis of blood cells and plasma to diagnose and monitor conditions such as anemia, infections, and clotting disorders.

BIOCHEMISTRY

We analyze the chemical composition of your blood to assess vital organ function, metabolic health, and overall well-being.

HISTOPATOLOGY

The microscopic examination of tissue samples (biopsies) to provide definitive diagnoses for a wide range of conditions, including cancer.

MICROBIOLOGY

Identifying the specific microorganisms—such as bacteria or viruses—causing an infection to guide effective and targeted treatment.
